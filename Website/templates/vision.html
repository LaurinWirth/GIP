The Mission behind 'Give in Public'

With Give In Public, we aim to rethink how we talk about altruism. The ultimate goal is to make giving easy and satisfying. To achieve this, we follow a three-step plan.


First, Give In Public aims to visualize the impact of your donations in one place. Think about Strava but than for your donations. The goal is to keep people who are already giving motivated and make them feel proud of the impact they have. Additionally, by making that impact public, it can multiply the impact you have by inspiring others to also do good (Some research about this effect here). In a nutshell, by being public about your donations, you can multiply your impact for free. Right now, we are focusing exclusively on building these features.


Second, after the beta, we will focus on making the giving process as easy as possible. To do that, we will let users pick cause areas they care about most. Based on these cause areas, we will select the most effective charities with the help of meta organisations such as GiveWell or Founders Pledge. The main reason behind this is to make giving easier for people that are not already donating.


Finally, we will work together with businesses to help them set up effective corporate social responsibility campaigns.
