FAQ

How do you measure the impact?

Measuring the precipice impact can be challenging. Our approach to succeed in this is to use existing data from meta evaluators and combine it with fixed metrics such as the total amount of money donated.

Can I already donate through Give In Public to automatically track my impact?

Right now, this is not possible. In the first step we want to get the tracking and visualization part of your impact right. Once we are happy with that, we will include ways to directly donate through Give In Public.

Any other questions?

If you have any other questions or ideas on how to improve Give In Public, please reach out to me via Twitter or Email.

Who is behind 'Give in Public'?

Hi, I am Laurin. After working for a tech company for the past years, I decided to take 12 months off to work on my own ideas. During these 12 months, I want to work on projects that contribute to a more equitable world. I believe that one way to achieve this goal is to better leverage technology in the non-profit sector. With ‘Give in Public’ the goal is to normalize giving and to multiply the impact everyone can have by making it public.


If you have some ideas or just want to connect, get in touch with me via Twitter or Email. Please also sign-up so I get a better understanding of what I need to build.
